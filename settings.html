RoutineClean Settings

Tab Duration
"Tab Duration" is how long a tab can be open before it is automatically closed. The default is 2 days.

Tab Whitelist
"Tab Whitelist" is the list of websites to exclude from closing due to expiration. Tabs with these websites will be excluded and remain open indefinitely. Enter a comma-separated list of domains to exclude from expiration (e.g. gmail.com, wunderlist.com, etc)!
